The Process

From idea to impact
in 4 simple steps

1

Student Submits a Suggestion

Students fill out a simple form — optionally anonymously. The AI chatbot VoiceBot helps them write a clear, impactful suggestion before submitting.

2

AI Instantly Analyzes It

The moment it's submitted, Gemini AI analyzes sentiment, suggests a category, assigns a priority, and generates a summary — automatically.

3

Admin Reviews & Responds

Admins see a full dashboard with smart filters, can assign to team members, draft AI-powered responses, and update the status with one click.

4

Student Gets Notified & Rates

A beautiful email lands in the student's inbox at every milestone. Once resolved, students rate their experience — closing the feedback loop.
